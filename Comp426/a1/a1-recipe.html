The Aaron Zhang Orange Creamsicle Recipe

Ingredients List:

For One Cocktail:

  • 2 Ounces of Vodka
  • 1/2 Ounce of Orange Liqeur (triple sec)
  • 4 Ounce of Orange Juice
  • 4 Ounce of Half and Half

For One Shot:

  • 1.5 Ounces of Vodka
  • 1/2 Ounce of Orange Liqeur (triple sec)
  • .5 Ounce of Orange Juice
  • .5 Ounce of Half and Half

Different Variations:

if you are in the mood for a tall, sweet Creamsicle, you have options.

Most recipes have three things in common: vodka, orange (juice or soda), and cream (ice cream, cream, or cream liqueur).

Boozy Creamsicle Float: Pour 1 shot of vanilla or whipped vodka and 1/2 shot triple sec into a chilled glass. Add a small scoop of vanilla ice cream and top with orange soda.

Simple Creamsicle: Pour a shot of vodka (Svedka Orange Cream Pop is a fun choice) with a shot of cream liqueur (RumChata's a good option) into an ice-cold glass and top with orange soda.

Instructions:

Orange Creamsicle Cocktail

  1. Pour the vodka and triple sec into a mixing glass filled with ice.
  2. Fill with equal parts of orange juice and half and half.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into a collins glass filled with fresh ice.

Orange Creamsicle Shot

  1. Pour the vodka and triple sec into a Shot glass.
  2. Fill with equal parts of orange juice and half and half.
  3. Drink.
